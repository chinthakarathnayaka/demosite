Higher National Diploma in Project Management


When was established HNDPM?
Project management is an unknown word for Sri Lanka, but it is not an unknown word for western countries. Project management starts from the most influential countries in the world like America. (Currently, even a Ph.D. degree in project management was offered in the USA)
Accordingly, Sri Lanka was first introduced to the University of Jaffna as a course in 2016 Also, this course has operated in the current Private University (NIBM, NSBM) in Sri Lanka and is limited to the rich class with money. Later in the year 2018, many people opened their eyes with the introduction of Project Management to the Kegalle Advanced Technological Institute.
Why should you apply?
A Project manager is a recognized person who performs a higher responsibility than an equivalent manager It's advantageous to have a project manager qualified for a job in every sector of a company. E.g.
  • Ability to become a government recognized project manager
  • Human Resources Manager
  • Project Planner
  • Scope Manager (Scope Stoke Manager)
  • Procurement Manager

You can apply for a job like this and also go abroad.
What will we be renderd for you?
The Project Management course which is currently running only at Kegalle Advanced Technological Institute is important to us because of the government's accredited Higher National Diploma (HND) certificate. Here, after training as Project Manager at 6 months recognized exam framework ( 2 years ) in a government university, the trails are opened to the job world.

What are qualifications you should have?
  • G.C.E. (A/L) passed all three subjects simultaneously, including the public test.
  • G.C.E. (O/L) Having a similar meaning (S) to the subject of English / Maths.

How do you apply HNDPM?
All duly filled applications should be directed to the relevant Advanced Technological Institute under a registered cover with a receipt obtained by crediting Rs 500 / - to the SLIATE account No 025-2-001-1-3397613 at peoples Bank, Park Street Branch (Colombo), or any other branch of Peoples Bank in Sri Lanka
