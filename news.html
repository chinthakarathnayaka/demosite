What was happened?

A sermon was prepared by the HNDIT department on Saturday, January 8, 2022 at 9 am.Lecturer Service of Sri Lanka Teacher Educators (National Faculty of Education, Nittambuwa).  Ven. Ampe Wimaladhamma Thero conducted the Dhamma Sermon.Due to the current situation in the country, it was broadcast live on YouTube, Facebook and Zoom technology.Students from HNDA, HNDE and HNDPM departments were also invited to participate in this sermon.In this way the sermon was successfully prepared and conducted by the HNDIT Department on that day.
